Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

At BiblioBaby, we are committed to protecting the privacy of children and families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Platform Data (TikTok/YouTube/Instagram)

  • We access only basic profile information provided by the platform (e.g., channel name, profile picture)
  • We do NOT collect personal information of minors
  • We do NOT track individual user behavior on children's accounts
  • We use platform APIs solely for content publishing purposes

Automatically Collected Information

  • Server logs (IP address, browser type, access times)
  • Device information for content optimization
  • Analytics data to improve content quality

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Publish gentle sensory video content to our channels
  • Improve content quality and relevance
  • Respond to user inquiries and support requests
  • Analyze platform performance metrics (views, engagement)
  • Ensure compliance with platform guidelines

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do NOT:

  • Sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Share data with data brokers
  • Use data for behavioral advertising targeting children

We may share data with:

  • Platform providers (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) as required for content publishing
  • Service providers who assist our operations (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Our Commitment:

  • Our content is designed for babies ages 0-3
  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Any data collection is done through parent/guardian accounts only
  • We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • We comply with GDPR-K (EU General Data Protection Regulation for children)

6. Cookies & Tracking

  • We use minimal essential cookies for basic functionality
  • We do NOT use tracking pixels or third-party tracking cookies
  • Platform providers (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) may use their own cookies

7. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services:

  • TikTok: Content publishing via TikTok for Developers API
  • YouTube: Content publishing via YouTube Data API v3
  • Instagram: Content publishing via Instagram Graph API

Each platform's privacy policy applies when using their services. Please review:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google/YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Secure API key management

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access what data we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

10. Data Retention

  • Platform data is retained only as long as necessary for publishing
  • Analytics data is anonymized after 90 days
  • Server logs are retained for 1 year for security purposes

11. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed and stored in:

  • European Union (primary location)
  • United States (for platform integration)

We ensure appropriate safeguards for international data transfers in compliance with GDPR requirements.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Continued use of services after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy. Significant changes will be notified via our platform.
